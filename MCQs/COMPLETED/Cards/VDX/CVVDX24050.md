2. **Heart failure** most commonly presents with **signs and symptoms of volume overload** and **normal cardiac output**.

6. **Low cardiac output** in heart failure is suggested by **hypotension**, **low pulse pressure**, **cool extremities**, and **reduced cognition**.
7. **Worsening kidney or liver function** in heart failure may be caused by **low cardiac output** or **vascular congestion**.
