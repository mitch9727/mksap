#### âœ… True Statements
1. **Pneumonia** is a **common cause of respiratory failure** in the intensive care unit (ICU).
2. **Pneumonia** is the **most common cause of acute respiratory distress syndrome (ARDS)** that develops outside the hospital.
3. In patients with an appropriate clinical history, **chest radiography** is the **standard diagnostic tool** for pneumonia.
4. A **negative clinical examination or chest radiograph** does **not exclude community-acquired pneumonia** in symptomatic patients, especially **older adults**.
5. The **absence of typical features or focal infiltrates** on chest radiograph should **not preclude early antibiotic administration** when the probability of pneumonia is otherwise high.
6. Some guidelines recommend **glucocorticoid administration** for **severe community-acquired pneumonia** in addition to antimicrobial therapy.

5. Early empiric antibiotics improve outcomes when pneumonia is strongly suspected, even without radiographic confirmation.
6. Glucocorticoid therapy in severe community-acquired pneumonia remains **guideline-dependent** and is not universally adopted.
