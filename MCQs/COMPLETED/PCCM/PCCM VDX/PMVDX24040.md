1. **Bronchiectasis** is defined as **irreversible dilatation of the bronchi**.
2. On chest CT, **bronchiectasis** is identified by **multiple dilated and thick-walled bronchi**.
3. The **signet ring sign** of bronchiectasis is present when the **diameter of a bronchus in cross-section is larger than the adjacent pulmonary artery**.
4. The **signet ring sign** appears as a **round or oval opacity** (dilated bronchus) adjacent to a **smaller opacity** (pulmonary artery).
5. In bronchiectasis, bronchi may show a **lack of normal tapering** in diameter when seen in **longitudinal section**.
6. **Mucus-filled bronchi** are a common finding in bronchiectasis.
7. Bronchiectasis may result from **airway damage due to chronic infection**.
8. **Difficulty in clearing mucus** from dilated bronchi contributes to **chronic colonization and infection**, perpetuating bronchiectasis.
