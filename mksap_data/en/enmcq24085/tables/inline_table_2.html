Hypoglycemia Management in a Patient With Diabetes Mellitus

Classification

Management Steps

Level 1 (<70 mg/dL [3.9 mmol/L])

Ingest 15 g of fast-acting carbohydrates

Repeat glucose measurement after 15 min; if glucose not >70 mg/dL (3.9 mmol/L), repeat treatment

Acute treatment should be followed by ingestion of long-acting carbohydrates

Consider adjusting insulin regimen

Level 2 (<54 mg/dL [3.0 mmol/L])

As above

Level 3 (altered mental and/or physical status that requires assistance for hypoglycemia treatment)

Bystander or medical professional provides treatment

IV access: 25 g of 50% glucose (dextrose)

No IV access: glucagon (preferred) or fast-acting carbohydrates (e.g., glucose gel)

Acute treatment should be followed by glucose infusion or ingestion of long-acting carbohydrates (if the patient is able to eat) with ongoing glucose monitoring

Adjust insulin regimen as needed
