Glucocorticoid Stress Dosing Strategies in Patients at Risk for Adrenal Insufficiency

Patient Risk

Patient Characteristics

Glucocorticoid Management

High risk

Established primary or secondary adrenal insufficiency

Cushingoid features

Current equivalent of >20 mg/d prednisone for >3 wk

Stress dosing

Moderate or possible risk

Past use equivalent of >20 mg/d prednisone for >3 wk in past year

Current or past equivalent of ≥5-20 mg/d prednisone for >3 wk during past year

Long-term high-dose inhaled or topical glucocorticoid therapy

Recent intra-articular glucocorticoid therapy (≥3 injectable glucocorticoid treatments within 3 mo before surgery)

Stress dosing or preoperative adrenal axis testing may be indicated

Low risk

Equivalent of <5 mg/d of prednisone for any duration

Any dose steroid for <3 wk

Low-dose inhaled or topical glucocorticoid therapy

No stress dosing
