Criteria for Lynch Syndrome a

Amsterdam II Criteria

Revised Bethesda Criteria b

≥3 relatives with Lynch-related tumor

CRC diagnosed at age <50 years

≥2 generations affected

Synchronous, metachronous Lynch-related tumors independent of age

1 cancer diagnosed at age <50 years

CRC with MSI-high–like histologic features c at age <60 years

1 relative is a first-degree relative of the other 2

CRC diagnosed in individual with ≥1 first-degree relative with Lynch-related tumor, if 1 was diagnosed at age <50 years

Familial adenomatous polyposis excluded

CRC diagnosed in individual with ≥2 first- or second-degree relatives with Lynch-related tumor, independent of age

Tumors confirmed by pathologic evaluation
