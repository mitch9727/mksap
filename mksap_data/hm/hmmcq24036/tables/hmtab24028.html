Thrombocytopenia

Platelet count decrease >50% and platelet nadir ≥20,000/μL (20 × 10 9 /L)

Platelet count decrease 30%-50% or platelet nadir 10,000-19,000/μL (10-19 × 10 9 /L)

Platelet count decrease <30% or platelet nadir <10,000/μL (10 × 10 9 /L)

Timing of platelet count decrease

Clear onset between days 5-10 or platelet count decrease ≤1 day (with heparin exposure within 30 days)

Consistent with platelet count decrease between days 5-10, but unclear (e.g., missing platelet counts); onset after day 10; or decrease ≤1 day (heparin exposure 30-100 days ago)

Platelet count decrease <4 days without recent exposure
