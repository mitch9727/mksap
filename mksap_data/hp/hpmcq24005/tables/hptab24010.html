Causes of Acute Liver Failure

Acetaminophen

Idiosyncratic drug-induced liver injury

Hepatitis A virus

Hepatitis B virus (with or without hepatitis D virus)

Hepatitis E virus (particularly in pregnant patients)

Herpes simplex virus, varicella zoster virus, cytomegalovirus (rare), Epstein-Barr virus (rare)

Autoimmune hepatitis

Wilson disease

Acute hepatic ischemia (e.g., systemic hypotension, Budd-Chiari syndrome)

Amanita phalloides mushrooms

Acute fatty liver of pregnancy

Hemolysis, elevated liver enzymes, and low platelet count (HELLP) syndrome
