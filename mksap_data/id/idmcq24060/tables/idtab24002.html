Empiric Treatment of Bacterial Meningitis

Clinical Characteristics

Empiric Antibiotic Regimen a

Immunocompetent adult age ≤50 y with community-acquired bacterial meningitis

IV ceftriaxone or cefotaxime plus IV vancomycin

Age >50 y or those with altered cell-mediated immunity

IV ampicillin ( Listeria coverage), plus IV ceftriaxone or cefotaxime, plus IV vancomycin b

Allergies to β-lactams

IV moxifloxacin instead of cephalosporin

IV trimethoprim-sulfamethoxazole instead of ampicillin

Health care–associated ventriculitis or meningitis

IV vancomycin plus either IV ceftazidime, cefepime, or meropenem
