Treatment of Recurrent Clostridioides difficile Infection

First recurrence (second episode)a

Preferred: Fidaxomicin, 200 mg twice daily PO × 10 d OR twice daily for 5 days followed by once every other day for 20 days

Alternative: Vancomycin, 125 mg four times daily PO × 10-14 d, then twice daily PO × 7 d, then once daily for 7 d, then every 2-3 d for 2-8 wk or

Alternative: Vancomycin, 125 mg four times daily PO × 10 d (consider if metronidazole was used for first episode)

Second and subsequent recurrence(s) (third and subsequent episodes)

Fidaxomicin, 200 mg twice daily PO × 10 d OR twice daily for 5 days followed by once every other day for 20 days

Vancomycin, 125 mg four times daily PO × 10-14 d, then twice daily PO × 7 d, then once daily for 7 d, then every 2-3 d for 2-8 wk or

Vancomycin, 125 mg four times daily PO × 10 d, followed by rifaximin, 400 mg three times daily PO × 20 d or

Fecal microbiota productsb or fecal microbiota transplantationc
