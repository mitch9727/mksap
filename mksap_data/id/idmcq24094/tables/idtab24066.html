Treatment of Initial Clostridioides difficile Infection

Severity of Disease a

Treatment b

Nonsevere

Fidaxomicin, 200 mg twice daily PO × 10 d (preferred)

Vancomycin, 125 mg four times daily PO × 10 d (alternative)

If neither oral fidaxomicin nor vancomycin is available (or both are contraindicated), metronidazole, 500 mg three times daily PO × 10-14 d

Severe

Fidaxomicin, 200 mg twice daily PO × 10 d (preferred)

Vancomycin, 125 mg four times daily PO × 10 d (alternative)

Fulminant

Vancomycin, 500 mg four times daily PO or by NGT, plus metronidazole, 500 mg every 8 h IV

If ileus is present, consider vancomycin 500 mg in approximately 100 mL of normal saline PR every 6 h

Assess all patients for indications for surgery
