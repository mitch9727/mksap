Classification of Abnormal Uterine Bleeding in Nonpregnant Women of Reproductive Age

Type

Description

Associated Causes

Ovulatory bleeding

Occurs at regular intervals

Menstrual flow is excessive

Thyroid disease, bleeding disorders, structural abnormalities (e.g., uterine fibroids, polyps, adenomyosis), obesity

Anovulatory bleeding

Irregular flow and cycle duration

Frequently occurs in the first few years after menarche and during perimenopause

Polycystic ovary syndrome, thyroid diseases, hyperprolactinemia, chronic diseases (e.g., chronic liver or kidney disease), obesity

Medications (e.g., antipsychotic agents, chemotherapy, tamoxifen)
