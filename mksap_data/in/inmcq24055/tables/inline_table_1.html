Testicular Workup for Ischemia and Suspected Torsion (TWIST) Score

Criteria

Testicular swelling (2 points)

Hard testis (2 points)

Absent cremasteric reflex (1 point)

Nausea or vomiting (1 point)

High-riding testis (1 point)

Interpretation

Score 0-2: Low risk; >90% negative predictive value for testicular torsion; generally no Doppler ultrasonography or urologic consultation required

Score 3-4: Intermediate risk; Doppler ultrasonography warranted

Score 5 or higher: High risk; >90% positive predictive value for torsion; Doppler ultrasonography not generally required; urgent urological consultation and surgery
