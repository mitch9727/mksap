Microhematuria: Stratification of Risk for Underlying Urothelial Malignancy

Low/Negligible Risk

Intermediate Risk

High Risk

Meet all of these criteria:

● Women age <60 years; men age <40 years

● Never-smoker or <10 pack-years smoking

● Urinalysis: 3 to 10 erythrocytes/hpf

● No additional urothelial cancer risk factors a

Meet ≥1 of these criteria:

● Women age ≥60 years; men age 40–59 years

● 10 to 30 pack-years smoking

● Urinalysis: 11 to 25 erythrocytes/hpf

● ≥1 additional urothelial cancer risk factors a

Meet ≥1 of these criteria:

● Men age ≥60 years; women not designated “high-risk” based only on age

● >30 pack-years smoking

● Urinalysis: >25 erythrocytes/hpf

● History of gross hematuria
