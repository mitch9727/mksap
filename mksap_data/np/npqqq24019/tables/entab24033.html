Case Detection Indications for Primary Aldosteronism

Untreated hypertension with sustained BP >150/100 mm Hg on three separate days

Resistant hypertension (>140/90 mm Hg) on three antihypertensives, including a diuretic

Controlled hypertension (<140/90 mm Hg) on four or more antihypertensives

Hypertension and an incidental adrenal mass

Hypertension associated with spontaneous or diuretic-induced hypokalemia

Hypertension and sleep apnea

Hypertension with a family history of either early-onset hypertension or cerebrovascular accident before age 40 years

Hypertension in the setting of a first-degree relative with PA
