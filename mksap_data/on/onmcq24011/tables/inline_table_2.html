Prostate Cancer Risk Stratification

Risk Category

Definition a

Very low/low

Stage T1-T2a AND serum PSA <10 ng/mL (10 µg/L) AND Grade Group 1 (Gleason 3+3)

Intermediate

T2b-T2c OR Grade Group 2-3 (Gleason score 7) OR PSA 10-20 ng/mL (10-20 µg/L)

High

T3a OR Grade Group 4-5 (Gleason score 8-10) OR PSA >20 ng/mL (20 µg/L)

Very high

T3b-T4 OR primary Gleason pattern 5 OR >4 cores with Grade Group 4-5 OR ≥2 high-risk features
