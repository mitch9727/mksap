Radiographic Characteristics and Staging in Pulmonary Sarcoidosis

Stage

Radiographic Pattern

Clinical Course and Comments

0

Normal

I

Hilar lymphadenopathy with normal lung parenchyma

>90% rate of spontaneous resolution without treatment

II

Hilar lymphadenopathy with abnormal lung parenchyma

Approximately 50% rate of spontaneous improvement without treatment

III

No lymphadenopathy with abnormal lung parenchyma

Approximately 20% rate of spontaneous improvement without treatment

IV

Parenchymal changes with fibrosis and architectural distortion
