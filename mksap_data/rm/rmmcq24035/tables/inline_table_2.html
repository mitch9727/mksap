Yamaguchi Criteria for Adult-Onset Still Disease

Major Criteria

Fever (temperature ≥39.0 °C [102.2 °F] for ≥1 weeks)

Arthralgia for ≥2 weeks

Salmon-colored macular/maculopapular rash during febrile episodes

Leukocyte count ≥10,000/μL (10 × 109/L), with ≥80% granulocytes

Minor Criteria

Sore throat

Lymphadenopathy or splenomegaly

Elevated aspartate aminotransferase, alanine aminotransferase, or lactate dehydrogenase levels

Negativity for antinuclear antibodies and rheumatoid factor
